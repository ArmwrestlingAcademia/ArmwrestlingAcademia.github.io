Upper Body

Elbows

Bicep Curl

A simple, supinated elbow flexor exercise to target the Bicep Brachii.

Assists general elbow strength, especially in a Hook.
Targeted Muscles

- Biceps Brachii

- Brachialis

- Forearm Supinator

Variation

Straight Barbell Bicep Curl

Either Standing or Seated, with a bar held in a supinated position (palms up) and elbows fully extended, flex the bicep and close the elbow joint, bring the forearm up to a full bicep contraction.

Video Example

Variation

Preacher Bicep Curl (Bar or Dumbbell)

Either Standing or Seated, with a dumbbell or barbell held in a supinated position (palms up), with the arms placed over the preacher bench and elbows almost fully extended*, flex the bicep and close the elbow joint, bring the dumbbell up to a full bicep contraction.

*Avoid reaching extreme extension of the bicep in this exercise as it will place excess stress on the bicep tendon while the bicep is in a weak position, leading to possible injury.

Variation

Scott Bicep Curl (Bar or Dumbbell)

Style 1: With the upper arm hung over the top of a bench or barbell, and the arm extended vertically downwards, with the dumbell or barbell in a supinated position (palms up), flex the bicep and close the elbow joint, bring the arm up to a full bicep contraction.

Style 2: With a dumbbell or barbell held in a supinated position (palms up), with the arms placed over the preacher bench and elbows almost fully extended*, flex the bicep and close the elbow joint, bring the dumbbell up to a full bicep contraction.

*Avoid reaching extreme extension of the bicep in this exercise as it will place excess stress on the bicep tendon while the bicep is in a weak position, leading to possible injury.

Variation

Concentration Bicep Curl

While seated, with the dumbell held between the knees in a supinated position (palms up) and the arm extended down vertically and the elbow or upper arm against the inside of the same side thigh, flex the bicep and close the elbow joint, bring the arm up to a full bicep contraction.

Forearms & Wrists

Wrist Curl / Finger Curl

A simple wrist flexion exercise to target the Flexors in the forearm.

Wrist Extension

A simple wrist extension exercise to target the Extensors in the forearm.

Wrist Pronation

Rotation of the forearm to face the palms of the hand downwards to target the pronators in the forearm.

Wrist Supination

Rotation of the forearm to face the palms of the hand upwards to target the biceps and supinators in the forearm.

Radial Deviation

Rotation of hand upwards to bring the thumb closer to the forearm to target the upper flexors and extensors in the forearm.

Ulnar Deviation

Rotation of hand downwards to bring the little finger closer to the forearm to target the lower flexors and extensors in the forearm.

Back

Row
